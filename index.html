About Me

I'm a computer enthusiast, deeply captivated by technology ever since my early experiences with Windows XP. Building my first gaming computer before turning 20 not only ignited my curiosity but also charted the course for my technical exploration.
 
Throughout diverse career challenges, I've consistently brought a positive and solution-driven mindset. Yet, I felt the pull towards a more technology-focused role. Heeding the encouragement from my peers and recognizing my passion, I pivoted my career path.
 
In 2023, I delved deep into software development, joining Tech Elevator's Java coding bootcamp. This immersive experience reinforced my love for collaborative problem-solving and paired programming. I'm proud of the growth I've achieved and am eager to bring my technical skills to new ventures.
 
I'm keen to connect with fellow tech professionals, explore collaborative opportunities, or simply discuss the latest in software development. Let's dive into enriching conversations and grow together!
