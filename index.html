About Me

Welcome to my Webpage! I'm a passionate computer enthusiast who has been captivated by technology since my early encounters with Windows XP. Building my first gaming computer before the age of 20 ignited my curiosity and set me on a path of exploration. Throughout my career, I've embraced diverse challenges and cultivated a positive outlook that has driven my success. However, I recently felt the need for something more fulfilling. Given the constant encouragement I've received to pursue a career in computers, I decided to make a change. In the spring of 2023, I took the initiative to join Tech Elevator's Java coding bootcamp. Throughout the program, I found great satisfaction in collaborative work and problem-solving through paired programming. It has been an incredible journey, and I look forward to forging new connections, continuous growth, and ongoing learning. I'm excited to connect with professionals in the industry and explore opportunities where I can contribute my technical skills and dedication to excellence. Let's connect and embark on this exciting journey together!
